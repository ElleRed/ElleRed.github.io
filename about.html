About Me

In case you were wondering :)

Hey all, it's Elle here! I'm so excited (and mildly embarrassed) that you've made it to my blog. I am very random with posting so who knows when my most recent post was, but feel free to look around and explore!

If you know me, you know I have a variety of interests, theories, and random fun facts floating around my head... and I love to go on rants. So I guess a blog is the least annoying way to get what's in this crazy brain of mine out for people to hear- well I guess see, in this case. At least you don't have to read it if you don't want to!

Just a few things about me: I am a Criminal Justice major currently attending Bob Jones University in Greenville, SC. I have a big family whom I love very dearly, and spend most of my time with.  My favorite hobbies are either watching movies with friends, singing in my car, learning ASL and most importantly doing Bible studies! Thanks for coming to my page, hope you enjoy the read.
